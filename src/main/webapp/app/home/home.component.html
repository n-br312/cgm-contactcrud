Patient Contact Details Management

Functionality:

  • Use "Patients" button in the navigation bar to access the list of contact details.
  • The table can be sorted via click on a table heading.
  • When creating a new patient ("create a new patient"-button) the firstname and surname has to be provided, the email will be checked for correct format. There can not be two patients with identical names.
  • The delete and edit function for a patient are provided after clicking on the "view"-button.
  • The filter function ("filter"-button), also checks if the search term is contained in the respective field of a patient, e. g. 'tim' as well as 'mo' would find a patient named 'Timo'.
  • The "reset filter"-button resets the filter and all patients will be displayed.
